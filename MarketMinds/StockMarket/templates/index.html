Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to some of the most common questions about MarketMinds and its features.

How can I view detailed stock information?

Simply navigate to the "View Stock Detail" section on the homepage, where you can enter the stock symbol to access comprehensive details, including price trends, historical data, and other relevant metrics.

Can I compare the performance of two different stocks?

Yes, you can compare two stocks by using the "Compare Two Stocks" feature. Enter the stock symbols for both stocks, and you'll see a side-by-side comparison of their performance, including key metrics and historical trends.

How accurate is the stock price prediction feature?

Our AI-powered stock price prediction feature is based on advanced algorithms and historical data. However, predictions are inherently uncertain and can be influenced by unforeseen market conditions. We advise users not to rely solely on these predictions for financial decisions and to consider other factors before making investments.
